Autumn in London

London is a wonderful city, especially in the fall. The red double-decker buses drive through the streets, and the parks are full of colorful leaves. The cool air and bright trees make it a perfect time for walks with friends.

In the fall, London feels a little magical, like Harry Potter stories. You can imagine the Hogwarts Express or Diagon Alley nearby. If you're a fan, the Warner Bros. Studio Tour is a great place to see movie sets and props.
